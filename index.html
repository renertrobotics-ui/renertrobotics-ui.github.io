Renert Robotics

Renert Robotics includes two FTC teams—31663 Cuttlefish Robotics and 27361 Octopi Robotics—and five FLL teams. Our students design, build, and program robots while developing skills in engineering and teamwork.

Learn About FTC

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a high school robotics program where students design, build, and code robots to compete in global engineering challenges. Our FTC teams thrive on collaboration and innovation.

Learn About FLL

FIRST LEGO League (FLL) introduces younger students to STEM through hands-on challenges using LEGO robots. It fosters critical thinking, teamwork, and creativity in a fun and engaging environment.
