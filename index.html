Renert Robotics

Renert’s cutting-edge robotics program is home to two competitive FTC teams: Octopi Robotics (Team 27361) and Cuttlefish Robotics (Team 31663). These student-driven teams design, build, and program robots to take on real-world engineering challenges in the global First Tech Challenge Competition. With a focus on creativity,collaboration, and technical excellence, Renert Robotics empowers future innovators to think big, solve problems, and lead with purpose.

Learn About FTC

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a high school robotics program where students design, build, and code robots to compete in global engineering challenges. Our FTC teams thrive on collaboration and innovation.

Learn About FLL

FIRST LEGO League (FLL) introduces younger students to STEM through hands-on challenges using LEGO robots. It fosters critical thinking, teamwork, and creativity in a fun and engaging environment.
