Our Mission

At Renert Robotics, we inspire students to explore STEM through hands-on robotics programs. Our teams gain experience in engineering, programming, teamwork, and problem-solving.

Our Teams

We host two FTC teams—31663 Cuttlefish Robotics and 27361 Octopi Robotics—and five FLL teams. Each team collaborates to design, build, and program robots for local and global competitions.

Community Outreach

Our students engage with the community by hosting workshops, mentoring younger teams, and participating in events that promote STEM education.
