Renert Robotics

Renert Robotics is a vibrant robotics program based at Renert School in Calgary. Our mission is to provide students with hands-on experience in engineering, coding, and teamwork through participation in FIRST competitions.

The program nurtures curiosity, innovation, and problem-solving skills for students of all ages.

Octopi Robotics – Team 27361

Octopi Robotics is our veteran FTC team competing in the 2025 season. Known for their creativity and engineering skill, the team focuses on drivetrain reliability, camera-based localization, and autonomous routines.

Their goal is to design competitive robots while mentoring new students and representing Renert Robotics with pride.

Cuttlefish Robotics – Team 31663

Cuttlefish Robotics is a rookie FTC team in 2025, diving into robotics with passion and innovation. They focus on creativity, teamwork, and intelligent robot design, bringing a fresh perspective to the Renert Robotics program.

Their current project is the second-generation robot, the Cuddler UWU, designed for speed, agility, and automation.
