About Renert Robotics

Renert Robotics includes two FTC teams—31663 Cuttlefish Robotics and 27361 Octopi Robotics—and multiple FLL teams. Our students design, build, and program robots while developing skills in engineering, coding, and teamwork.

27361 Octopi Robotics

Octopi Robotics is our veteran FTC team, focusing on engineering excellence, autonomous programming, and mentoring new students. They strive to create competitive robots and a supportive learning environment.

31663 Cuttlefish Robotics

Cuttlefish Robotics is our rookie FTC team, bringing creativity and fresh ideas. Students focus on teamwork, coding, and innovative robot designs while learning engineering fundamentals.

Renert Robotics Program

Renert Robotics nurtures students from elementary to high school through FTC and FLL programs. We emphasize problem-solving, collaboration, and innovation, giving students hands-on experience in STEM and robotics competitions.
