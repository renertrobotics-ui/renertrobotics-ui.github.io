Octopi Robotics (Team 27361)

Octopi Robotics is a student-driven FTC team based at Renert. Our members collaborate to design, build, and program innovative robots that tackle real engineering challenges.

Through the FIRST Tech Challenge, our team learns problem-solving, coding, engineering design, and teamwork — preparing us to be the next generation of innovators.

We are proud to represent our school and community in competitions and outreach, sharing our passion for STEM with others.
