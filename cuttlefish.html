About Us

We are a team comprised of 8 grade 9 and 10 students at Renert School that are passionate about robotics. In 2025, we formed 31663 Cuttlefish Robotics at Renert School, with the intention of becoming a world-class team! We are greatly indebted to our mentors, Mr. Christian, and Mr. Wilcox! Go Renert!
